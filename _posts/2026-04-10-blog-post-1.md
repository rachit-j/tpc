## Technological Manifesto

As a technical writer who specializes in AI, Robotics, and Cybersecurity, which are all areas where offensives can be taken and people can be impacted, I understand that the words I write have the potential to alter lives significantly. I commit to using my craft responsibly and ethically, with certain lines I will not cross. Reading *Understand* by Ted Chiang showed me the implications of overusing technology and knowledge, especially with extreme manipulation. With that in mind, I would like to lay down my ethics for technical writing with a clear goal: I will not seek to create products that artificially change or manipulate humanity for my benefit. While I recognize that there may be operations that I must perform that may impact other individuals, I will not use that power purely for my benefit. I also seek to write clearly and without manipulation, as my work should promote understanding and intellectual conversation, not obfuscation. I'm okay with guiding actions both offensively and defensively as long as innocent people are not purposefully targeted by my work. These principles of working towards benefits for those around me without manipulation, being clear and intentional in my writing, and guiding ideas that do not target innocent people are part of my core values in technical and professional writing.
